How to Make Shrimp Tempura

Description

Craving sushi but don't want to break the bank? Why don't we trying making our own? Let's go!

Ingredients & Materials

  • Sushi Mat
  • Plastic Wrap
  • Shrimp Tempura (store-bought)
  • Sushi Rice**
  • Cucumber
  • Avocado
  • Nori (seaweed)
  • Tobiko (flying fish roe)
  • Unagi Sushi Sauce
**Refer to sushi rice recipe on homepage

Steps

  1. Bake the shrimp according to package directions
  2. Opt: Put a piece of plastic wrap over sushi mat
  3. Add a nori sheet on top
  4. Wet your heads and spread rice into an even layer on top
  5. Finish with a sprinkle of tobiko on top
  6. Flip the nori to the other side
  7. Add rest of ingredients on this side (shrimp, cucumber, and avocado)
  8. Tightly roll it up using sushi mat
  9. Wet a sharp knife under cold water & cut the roll into 8 pieces
  10. Drizzle with eel sauce & enjoy!
