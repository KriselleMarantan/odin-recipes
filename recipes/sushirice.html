How to Make Sushi Rice

Description

The perfect rice recipe for the perfect sushi rolls!

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Rice Vinegar
  • Sugar
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Follow the steps on how to make rice recipe located on homepage
  2. When done, transfer the cooked rice to a baking sheeet or large skillet.
  3. Cut it up using a spoon
  4. Make the rice seasoning by mixing rice vinegar, sugar, and salt in a small bowl
  5. Heat it in the microwave for a few seconds to dissolve the sugar
  6. Pour the vinegar mixture all over the rice to season
  7. Mix it together well
  8. Leave it to cool.
  9. This can now be used to make delicious sushi with! (Might I recommend a good shrimp tempupra recipe from the homepage?)
